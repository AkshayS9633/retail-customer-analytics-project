# Customer Analytics: Segmentation & Market Basket Analysis

##  Overview
This project analyzes transactional data to uncover customer behavior patterns and provide actionable business insights. It includes two analytical components:
1. Customer Segmentation using transactional data
2. Market Basket Analysis using POS data

The analysis supports targeted marketing, personalized offers, and revenue optimization.
---
Datasets are stored in the `data/` directory.
---

##  Part A: Customer Segmentation (Automobile Parts Company)

### Objective
- Identify purchasing patterns from 3 years of transaction data
- Segment customers based on buying behavior
- Recommend targeted marketing strategies for each segment

### Approach
- Transactional data analysis
- Feature engineering (sales, frequency, recency)
- Customer segmentation using KNIME
- Insight generation for business decisions

### Workflow
 part_a_customer_segmentation.knwf

---

##  Part B: Market Basket Analysis (Grocery Retail POS Data)

### Objective
- Identify frequently purchased item combinations
- Enable combo offers and cross-selling strategies
- Increase average basket size and revenue

### Approach
- POS data preprocessing
- Association rule mining
- Evaluation using support, confidence, and lift
- Business recommendations for promotions

### Workflow
  part_b_market_basket_analysis.knwf
---
##  Tools & Technologies
- KNIME Analytics Platform
- Transactional & POS data
- Data preprocessing & analytics

##  Key Outcomes
- Identified distinct customer segments for personalized marketing
- Discovered frequent product combinations for targeted promotions
- Delivered actionable insights aligned with business goals
